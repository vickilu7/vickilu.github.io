Blueprint Conference

Work   Play   About   Resume

Blueprint is Duke's sustainable tech ideation conference presented by Duke Conservation Tech. We bring together passionate students, visionary experts, and forward-thinking organizations to address critical environmental issues. 2018 was the second year it ran, and I was extremely excited and honored to be the graphic designer for the entire conference.

The Color Palette

I went for a family of nature element colors (blues + greens) along with an earthy red accent as well as a neutral white

The Logo

The Blueprint logo features a simple paw-print outline with a fingerprint pattern fill that represents the intersection between human impact and the natural environment.

2018 Theme: Nature + Progress

Nature + Progress focuses on reconciling the natural world with the inevitability of human progress. Humans have exploited ecosystems to support growing societal demands. While beneficial in the short term, these disruptions have led to a widespread degradation of ecosystem services, the often overlooked things nature does for us.

Wave graphic incorporate arrow to show this "progress"
