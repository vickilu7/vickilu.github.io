IDEATE
Design Team
for HackDuke

IDEATE is Duke's Design Thinking & Innovation Conference, founded by HackDuke (our hackathon organization). As part of the HackDuke Design Team, I was especially excited to organize this day-long conference filled with speakers and workshops to explore how design and technology interact. 2018 was our third year running the conference.

Rebrand

This year,we decided on a navy and peach combo for the color scheme as well as had a design challenge for participants. We liked the idea of combining grids and squares with more flowing visual elements to represent the union of technological organization with design creativity.
