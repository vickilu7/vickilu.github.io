Mycometrics

Mycometrics is a microbiology services laboratory specialized in the identification of fungi (molds), bacteria and viruses from the environment, food, pharmaceutical or biological products. They were established to provide unique services, combining the use of both conventional and emerging technologies, and employing the highest standards available.

They're currently building a new version of their website and wanted an updated logo that matched the overall feel. My design incorporates traditional lab elements with the petri dish and microbe, but still preserves a minimalistic and clean look like the website. MPower

A team of 4 students at Duke created mPower, a startup aiming to fill India’s shortage of agricultural cold storage solutions by offering a novel product and distribution network that compensates farmers and simplifies the supply chain. mPower won the Hult Prize @ Duke as well as one of 14 regional rounds across the world in Mexico City. They are now headed to the Hult finals held in London. The Hult Prize, the world’s largest social entrepreneurship competition for college students, advertises itself as the “Nobel Prize for social entrepreneurs.”

I was asked to show the energy-saving and environemental aspect of the startup in their logo and, ideally, combine fruit/crops/plants visually. I created different versions of logo possibilities showing this.

Codeable Camps

Codeable Camps strives to teach the younger generation the basic concepts behind programming in a way that is not only educational but also extremely engaging and interesting. Through the use of Scratch, an open source programming language produced by MIT, children of all ages are able to receive an early exposure to programming while developing games, animations, and many other applications in an intuitive and fun environment. The Codeable Camps logo features colorful blocks that are supposed to represent the action blocks used in Scratch programming.


Entropy

How can we make people mindful of the things they put out into the universe in an engaging way? We want to make being environmental more personal. People these days want to see their own individual impact, and Entropy is a way to do just that. With this personal dashboard, people are reminded of their daily habits and are brought face-to-face with their impact. I used a lot of nature elements for this project at Blueprint: Duke's sustainability conference. Below are some graphics used for the social aspect of Entropy– seeing your friends' plants to keep each other environmentally accountable.


Blue Devils United

Blue Devils United is the student group for LGBTQ undergraduate students, allies, and friends. Blue Devils United seeks to provide social opportunities for LGBTQ students and their allies, outreach to students at Duke and in the community, and to advocate for the needs of LGBTQ individuals both at Duke and beyond.
