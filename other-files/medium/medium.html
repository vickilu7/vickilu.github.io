Medium App New Feature Concept

Work   Play   About   Resume

Let me first start by saying that Medium is one of my absolute three favorite services created (the other two being Spotify and Typeform). With its minimalistic and sophisticated UI as well as intuitive UX, the Medium app is nearly perfect in my eyes. However, as a user, I’ve found myself wishing it did have a more extensive bookmarking system.

Description of Project

Problem: As of now, you can only either save articles you liked to your “Bookmarks” or to your “Claps”– offering only two lists to differentiate your articles.
Solution: Create a feature that allows users to categorize their favorite articles into different categories. Think Pinterest boards or Youtube playlists! In the case of Medium, I decided to categorize the bookmarks into “shelves”.

Process and Work

Inspiration
These “shelves” act as the topics in which users can sort their articles, much like how you’d place similar genre books near each other on a bookshelf. The way the bookmarking looks on the home page is heavily inspired by Airbnb. I felt that the clean and straightforward look of the Save to List modal window fit in quite well with Medium’s overall brand.
